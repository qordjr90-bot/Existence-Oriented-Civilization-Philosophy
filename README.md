Existence-Oriented Civilization Philosophy

    Orientation

Existence → Better Existence
Civilization is not defined by growth, accumulation, or competition.
It is a process through which existence sustains itself, improves its quality, and expands its interaction with reality.

    Core
    
Civilization should be evaluated by:
stability of existence
quality of experience
continuity across time
expansion of capability and interaction
All other systems are instrumental.
